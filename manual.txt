STICKER SPINNER MANUAL =>

three rows of keys to control everything!

numbers = stickers
qwerty row = sticker movement routine
asdf row = background routine

PERFORMANCE TRICKS:

space bar = turns off screen redraw letting things blend (doesnt work well on everythingonly negative space stuff)
up and down = moves between backgrounds in sequence
left and right = moves between stickers in sequence
